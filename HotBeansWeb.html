Job Specifications & Required Qualifications

Front-End Developer

Hot Beans Web is seeking a skilled Front-End Developer to join our team. The ideal candidate will have a strong command of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, with experience in modern JavaScript frameworks such as React or Angular. This role involves creating visually appealing and user-friendly interfaces that enhance user experience.

Responsibilities:

  • Develop and maintain front-end code for web applications.
  • Collaborate with design teams to translate UI/UX designs into interactive interfaces.
  • Ensure the technical feasibility of UI/UX designs.
  • Optimize applications for maximum speed and scalability.
  • Conduct thorough testing of web applications to identify and resolve bugs.

Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or a related field.
  • Proven experience as a Front-End Developer or similar role.
  • Proficiency in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
  • Experience with React, Angular, or similar frameworks.
  • Strong understanding of responsive design principles.
  • Excellent problem-solving skills and attention to detail.

Back-End Developer

Hot Beans Web is looking for an experienced Back-End Developer to build and maintain server-side logic, ensuring high performance and responsiveness to requests from the front-end. The ideal candidate will have expertise in server-side languages and database management.

Responsibilities:

  • Develop and manage server-side components and APIs.
  • Integrate user-facing elements developed by front-end developers.
  • Optimize applications for maximum speed and scalability.
  • Implement data security and protection.
  • Conduct unit and integration testing.

Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or a related field.
  • Proven experience as a Back-End Developer or similar role.
  • Proficiency in server-side languages such as Node.js, Python, or PHP.
  • Experience with database technologies like SQL, MySQL, and MongoDB.
  • Strong understanding of RESTful APIs and web services.
  • Excellent problem-solving skills and attention to detail.

Full-Stack Developer

Hot Beans Web seeks a versatile Full-Stack Developer proficient in both front-end and back-end technologies. The ideal candidate will have a comprehensive understanding of web development processes and the ability to handle multiple aspects of web application development.

Responsibilities:

  • Develop and maintain both client-side and server-side components.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to define and design new features.
  • Ensure high performance and responsiveness of applications.
  • Troubleshoot and debug applications.
  • Conduct thorough testing of web applications to identify and resolve bugs.

Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or a related field.
  • Proven experience as a Full-Stack Developer or similar role.
  • Proficiency in front-end technologies (HTML, CSS, JavaScript) and frameworks (React, Angular).
  • Proficiency in back-end technologies (Node.js, Python, PHP) and databases (SQL, MySQL, MongoDB).
  • Strong understanding of web development processes and best practices.
  • Excellent problem-solving skills and attention to detail.
